About AgriTrace

Building a future of trust and transparency in agriculture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers by creating a simple, secure, and transparent food traceability system. We leverage technology to empower farmers, inform consumers, and ensure the integrity of the food supply chain from farm to table.

What We Do

Empower Farmers

We provide farmers with easy-to-use digital tools to register their crops, creating a permanent, verifiable record of their hard work.

Inform Consumers

Through a simple QR code scan, consumers get instant access to the story behind their foodâ€”its origin, journey, and quality.

Ensure Authenticity

By using secure hash verification, we ensure that the data from the farm is authentic and has not been tampered with, combating food fraud.
